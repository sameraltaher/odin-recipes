Mac 'n Cheese, A Timeless Classic.

Description

Ahh. Smells like.. home. Little bit carb, little bit fat, kind of really salty. Need I say more?

Any mac n cheese that comes straight out of a box will work with this recipe. We use whole milk but lower fat content milk can be used. You can also swap the butter with margarine or skip it entirely.

Ingredients

  • Annie's Penne and Four Cheese Mac 'n Cheese
  • 11⁄2 tbsp un-salted butter
  • 5-6 cups water
  • 3 tbsp whole milk.

Steps

Prep time: 30 seconds
Cook time: 8 - 10 minutes

  3. Add the macaroni (or other pasta) and boil at medium-high for 7-9 minutes (7 minutes for al dente).
  4. Drain the past and set aside in a warm bowl. and save the pasta water in case you needed to make more mac n cheese (why not).
  5. Put the pot back on the stove and turn off heat.
  6. Melt the butter in the same pot.
  7. Add the milk.
  8. Open the dried cheese packet and sprinkly on the milky buttery goodness.
  9. Stir gently and combine well.
  10. Mix the pasta into the cheese sauce.
  11. Serve and enjoy!
